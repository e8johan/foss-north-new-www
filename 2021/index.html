Announcing foss-north 2021

foss-north is a free / open source conference covering both software and hardware from the technical perspective. Hosted in Gothenburg between Copenhagen, Oslo, and Stockholm, with an international airport, we provide a meeting place for the Nordic foss communities and will bring together great speakers with a great audience.

The conference will be held on May 31 - June 1, paired with the community day on May 30. Due to the ongoing pandemic, the event will be virtual.

foss-north 2021 is over - thank you for everyone who spoke and attended!

Call for Projects

The call for projects for the community day is open until May 15. For more information, visit the Contribute page.
