What?

foss-north is a free / open source conference covering both software and hardware from the technical perspective. Hosted in Gothenburg between Copenhagen, Oslo and Stockholm with an international airport, we provide a meeting place for the Nordic foss communities and will bring together great speakers with a great audience.
 
Tickets are available from the tickets page.

When and Where?

foss-north 2018 will be hosted at Chalmers Gibraltargatan, Gothenburg on April 22-23.
We plan one and a half days, starting 13:00 - 17:00 on Sunday followed by a full day, 8:00 - 17:00, on Monday.
 
 
 

Hotel

Gothenburg is known for the many events in the city and there are plenty of hotels available. We have arranged for a block of discounted rooms at Scandic Rubinen at the heart of the city. Details can be found on the tickets page.
